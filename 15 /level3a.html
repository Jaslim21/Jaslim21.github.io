THE CULT OF DONE

Bre Pettis and Kio Stark




There are three states of being. Not knowing action and completion.

Accept that everything is a draft. It helps to get done.

There is no editing stage.

Pretending you know what you’re doing is almost the same as knowing what you are doing,
so just accept that you know what you’re doing even if you don’t and
 do it.
